About the Summit

The Puerto Rico Sports Performance Summit is the premier gathering for athletes, trainers, and innovators who want to push the boundaries of human performance. Our mission is to inspire and empower attendees by showcasing cutting-edge techniques, tools, and research.

During this full-day event you'll experience hands-on demonstrations, presentations by industry leaders, and opportunities to network with peers and experts. Whether you're an elite competitor or a passionate enthusiast, the summit is designed to help you take your performance to the next level.

Join us on May 16, 2026, and be part of a community committed to excellence in sports performance.
