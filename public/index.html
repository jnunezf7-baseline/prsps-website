About PRSPS

The Puerto Rico Sports Performance Summit (PRSPS) is the premier educational event in the Caribbean focused on sports medicine, rehabilitation and performance science. Our mission is to elevate Puerto Rico as a hub for sports performance education, connecting local and international professionals and promoting the island as a destination for learning and tourism.
