La Beca José González

In honour of trailblazing Puerto Rican professor of kinesiology José González, PRSPS partners with the Universidad del Sagrado Corazón to award an internship scholarship each year. The recipient of La Beca José González will travel to the United States for a two‑week clinical experience in sports medicine and rehabilitation.

About the Internship

The selected student will have the opportunity to gain hands‑on experience by working alongside clinicians, researchers and athletic trainers in sports medicine and rehabilitation. Specific placement details will be arranged with our partners. Travel and lodging assistance is provided through the scholarship, however full coverage of expenses cannot be guaranteed.

Eligibility & Application

  • Applicants must be enrolled in a programme at the Universidad del Sagrado Corazón.
  • Applicants should demonstrate academic excellence and a strong interest in sports medicine or rehabilitation science.
  • Submit a CV, letter of intent and two references to the Scholarship Committee by the specified deadline.

Application details and deadlines will be announced through university channels. If you have questions about the scholarship, please contact info@baseline-pt.com.
